The Student Kitchen

Our vision is to empower NYC youth to take autonomy over their food and their communities.

What We Do

The Student Kitchen is an after-school program for New York City students grades K-12 that uses cooking workshops to build healthy habits, grow students' confidence in the kitchen, and explore new cultures.
