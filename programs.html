Skills & Safety Training

  • Practice proper knife technique
  • Learn to use different kitchen utensils and equipment
  • Gain confidence in cooking for themselves and others for meals in cheaper, healthier ways than eating out
  • Grow and develop outside of traditional academic spaces, including creativity with flavors, self-confidence, and patience

Kitchen Protocol

  • Communicate end-set safety in a busy kitchen
  • Learn ServSafe food handling skills, including handling produce, raw meat and shellfish, cooking to proper internal temperatures, and hygienic practices in serving food
  • Practice working within the grocery, where we practice working with a budget and making healthy choices

Cultural Competency

  • Learn about cultures from around the world alongside guests with deep cultural and culinary knowledge
  • Be exposed to the stories, specialty ingredients, and cultural context behind the meals that they cook
  • Interact with the world through food
